About The Show

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror drama television series created by the visionary Duffer Brothers (Matt and Ross Duffer). Set in the nostalgic 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, the show masterfully follows a group of kids who encounter supernatural forces and secret government exploits that will change their lives forever.

When young Will Byers mysteriously vanishes on November 6, 1983, the small town of Hawkins uncovers a terrifying mystery involving secret experiments conducted by Hawkins National Laboratory, supernatural forces from an alternate dimension called "The Upside Down," and one extraordinary little girl with psychokinetic abilities known only as "Eleven."

The series is a brilliant tribute to 1980s pop culture, drawing inspiration from the legendary works of Steven Spielberg, John Carpenter, Stephen King, and George Lucas. From the iconic synthesizer soundtrack by Kyle Dixon and Michael Stein to the authentic period details, every element transports viewers back to the golden age of sci-fi and horror.

Main Characters

Alive

Eleven (Jane Ives)

Millie Bobby Brown

A young girl with psychokinetic abilities who escaped from Hawkins National Laboratory. Known as Subject 011, she has the power to move objects with her mind and access other dimensions. Adopted by Jim Hopper and later reunited with her biological mother.

Powers: Telekinesis, Remote Viewing, Dimensional Travel

Mike Wheeler

Finn Wolfhard

The heart and leader of the group, Mike is Will's best friend and Eleven's boyfriend. He's fiercely loyal, intelligent, and always ready to protect his friends. The strategist of the Party who often comes up with plans to fight supernatural threats.

D&D Class: Paladin (The Heart)

Dustin Henderson

Gaten Matarazzo

The comic relief and tech genius of the group with cleidocranial dysplasia. Dustin is curious, resourceful, and has an infectious personality. He discovers and initially cares for a baby Demogorgon he names D'Artagnan (Dart).

D&D Class: Bard (The Comic Relief)

Lucas Sinclair

Caleb McLaughlin

The skeptical and practical member of the group who always questions everything. Lucas is an excellent shot with his wrist rocket slingshot and later becomes Max's boyfriend. He's protective of his friends and sister Erica.

Noah Schnapp

The sensitive artist of the group whose disappearance in Season 1 kickstarted the entire series. After being trapped in the Upside Down, Will developed a connection to the dimension and can sense when it's near. He's also struggling with his sexuality.

Stranger Things Timeline

1959
Hawkins National Laboratory Founded
The Department of Energy establishes Hawkins Lab as part of the nuclear energy program, though it secretly conducts MKUltra experiments on children with psychokinetic abilities.
1970
Henry Creel Becomes 001
Young Henry Creel, after murdering his family, is taken to Hawkins Lab and becomes the first test subject. Dr. Brenner begins studying his remarkable psychic abilities.
1979
The Massacre at Hawkins Lab
Henry Creel (001) manipulates Eleven into removing his Soteria implant. He kills nearly everyone in the lab, but Eleven banishes him to the Upside Down, where he becomes Vecna.
November 6, 1983
Will Byers Disappears
Will Byers vanishes while cycling home from a D&D campaign. A Demogorgon takes him to the Upside Down. This date becomes frozen in the alternate dimension forever.
November 7, 1983
Eleven Escapes the Lab
Eleven escapes from Hawkins Lab and encounters Mike, Dustin, and Lucas. She reveals the existence of the Upside Down and helps them search for Will.
November 12, 1983
The Demogorgon is Defeated
Eleven sacrifices herself to defeat the Demogorgon at Hawkins Middle School. Will is found alive but infected with particles from the Upside Down.
October 29, 1984
The Mind Flayer Emerges
Will's connection to the Upside Down allows the Mind Flayer to possess him. The shadow monster begins spreading through Hawkins' underground tunnel system.
November 3, 1984
The Gate is Closed
Eleven closes the original gate to the Upside Down. The Mind Flayer is expelled from Will's body, and the immediate threat ends. The Snow Ball dance celebrates normalcy.
June 28, 1985
Russians Open a New Gate
Soviet scientists successfully open a new gate to the Upside Down beneath Starcourt Mall, reactivating the Mind Flayer and its influence over the possessed.
July 4, 1985
The Battle of Starcourt
The Mind Flayer creates a massive flesh monster. Hopper sacrifices himself to close the Russian gate. Billy Hargrove dies protecting Eleven. The Byers family moves away.
March 21, 1986
Vecna's Murders Begin
Henry Creel, now known as Vecna, begins killing teenagers in Hawkins through psychic attacks. Each murder creates a new gate, weakening the barrier between dimensions.
March 27, 1986
The Four Gates Open
After Max's near-death experience, Vecna successfully opens four gates that converge in the center of Hawkins, creating a massive rift and beginning the apocalypse.

Main Villains

The Upside Down

What is the Upside Down?

The Upside Down is a parallel dimension existing in tandem with the human world. It's a dark, decaying version of Hawkins with floating particles in the air, a perpetually overcast sky, and an abundance of fleshy vines covering the landscape.

The dimension is home to various creatures, including Demogorgons and the Mind Flayer. The atmosphere is toxic to humans, requiring protective gear for extended exposure.

How to Enter the Upside Down

There are several known ways to access the Upside Down:

  • Through naturally occurring gates (rare)
  • Via artificially created gates by Eleven or the Hawkins Lab
  • Through temporary portals created by certain creatures
  • By being taken there by a Demogorgon

The dimension seems to have some connection to Eleven's powers and the experiments conducted at Hawkins National Laboratory.

Creatures of the Upside Down

The Upside Down is inhabited by several dangerous creatures:

  • Demogorgons: Humanoid predators with flower-like faces
  • Demodogs: Younger, dog-like versions of Demogorgons
  • The Mind Flayer: A shadowy entity that controls other creatures
  • Vecna: A powerful humanoid with psychic abilities
